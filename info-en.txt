AMD Catalyst driver fix for laptops/tablets.

This installer repairs the AMD Catalyst driver bug that prevents display brightness adjustments on laptops/tablets based on the A3/A4/A6/A8/A10 APU family. The bug only affects Windows 8/8.1/10

The installer modifies only a registry key.

After setup restart is required. After restart the brightness adjustments should work again. After driver updtates re-run the installer.
